<h1 align="center"> Excel Project With Sales Data of Cafe for the Year 2023</h1> 

This data has been downloaded from Kaggle using the following link:  

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ahmedmohamed2003/cafe-sales-dirty-data-for-cleaning-training . 

This data has eight columns and 10,000 rows and covers the annual sales for a cafe for the year 2023. The columns cover the sales of items sold, price of each item, quantity of each item sold, the total money spent on each sale, payment method used (cash, credit card or digital wallet), location (whether the items bought were for in-store consumption or takeaway) and the transaction date is given.  

<h2 align="center"> 🎯 Objectives  </h2> 
This data has been used to practise and display Excel proficiency. The objectives of this project are:  

* To clean data in Excel 
* To fill out missing values in the data from the data given 
* To assess data quality of the cleaned data 
* To make pivot tables 
* To create a dashboard in order in see trends and gain insights from the given data

<h2 align="center"> 📊 Creating Dashboard  </h2>   

The dashboard visualises the most sold item, total revenue generated and the total annual sales in the cafe. I have created visualisations to show that the total revenue generated by customers who consumed food on the cafe premises and who took take-away. The other visualisations are the revenue generated by each payment type (credit card, cash or digital wallet), Total revenue generated by item, total revenue generated each month and total monthly revenue generated by each item. Since I spent time cleaning the data, I wanted to assess the quality of the data as well. The raw data had blanks, cells with "UNKNOWN" and cells with "ERROR" in them. These cells were 10.082 in total. After cleaning and filling some of the missing data using the information in other cells, this missing data in cells were reduced to 8651 as shown in the dashboard. This missing data can skew the analysis.

<h2 align="center"> 💡 Key Insights  </h2>  

- Salad is the most popular item generating the most revenue every month. It creates the most revenue whether the payment method is cash, credit card or the digital wallet. The next two items are sandwich and smoothie.
- Most payments are made by credit card, then digital wallet and then cash.
- A larger number of customer prefer toeat in-store.
- The total monthly revenue is quite stable. The trendline on the revenue generated is horizontal showing stable sales over the months apart from February where the sales dip. The month over month percentage change shows oscillation between growth and contraction. Although the revenue generated is stable, there's no long-term trend or growth.
